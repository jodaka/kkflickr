KKFlickr options

Tags

You can supply as many tags as you want. They will be added to all the photos you upload. Default: KKF

  • KKF

Pictures size

If you upload picture which width is below selected option, it will be inserted without resizing.

Privacy

All uploaded pictures would be marked as "hidden" and would not appear in public searches. However you can show them in your photostream using "Public" settings, or keep them visible only to you (default option).

Settings are saved automatically
